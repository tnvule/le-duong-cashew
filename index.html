QUALITY ASSURANCE

Controlled from raw material to sealed carton.

Separate checkpoints protect appearance, moisture, food safety, packing integrity and traceability.

01

Raw material screening

Moisture, size and visible contamination are checked before processing.

02

Controlled processing

Steam, shelling and drying parameters are monitored by trained operators.

03

Grade & color sorting

Kernels are separated by size, color, breakage and defect tolerance.

04

Food-safety verification

Microbiology, aflatoxin and heavy-metal testing can be arranged to order.

05

Packing inspection

Metal detection, vacuum sealing, labels and carton integrity are verified.

06

Lot traceability

Production lots, retain samples and shipment records support every order.
